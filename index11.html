Important Information

Acceptable documents may include custodian/broker statements, clearing house blotters, or other independent, third-party verification and/or confirmation of the transactions and/or holding. The statements must contain all of the following information:

  • Account Name
  • An Account identifier (account number or other ID)
  • Security identifier (CUSIP/SEDOL/ISIN and security name)
  • Transaction date/holding date
  • Transaction type (buy/sell/corporate action)
  • Quantity of shares
  • Transaction price and gross/net amount

Please note: If you are providing a letter from a custodian/broker, the letter must include all of the information above as well as the following:

  • Letter must be on custodian/broker letterhead
  • Letter must include Contact Name and contact information (phone number and email) of authorized representative
  • DocuSign by Authorized representative
  • Must be either 1) Notarized or 2) Medallion Stamped
